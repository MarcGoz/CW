Lower Prices, Raise Wages Campaign

Our campaign aims to promote positive change in the world by urging the regime to increase the minimum wage for our people. The current minimum wage is not enough to cover basic needs and many workers are struggling to make ends meet. By raising the minimum wage, we can reduce poverty, promote economic growth and create a fairer society.

How You Can Help

There are several things you can do to support our campaign:

  • Educate yourself and others: Learn more about the issue and share your knowledge with others. Use your social media platforms to raise awareness and encourage others to take action.
  • Join our rallies and protests: Show your support by joining our rallies and protests. Together, our voices can make a difference.
  • Sign our petition: Sign our petition to urge the regime to increase the minimum wage for our people.
  • Contact your elected officials: Contact your elected officials and urge them to support policies that promote fair wages for all workers. Vote for politicians who prioritize social issues.

Together, we can make a positive impact on the world and create a fairer society. Join us in taking action today.

Thank you for your support.
